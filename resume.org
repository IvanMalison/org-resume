#+BEGIN_QUOTE
I am an associate professor at the University of Paris-South, doing my research
application at the Laboratoire de l'Accélérateur Linéaire.

I am interested in neutrino physics, looking for Majorana neutrino and thus
searching for neutrinoless double-beta decay. I also contribute to and maintain
some open source projects on [[https://github.com/xgarrido][GitHub]].
#+END_QUOTE
